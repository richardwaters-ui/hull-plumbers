Common Questions

Got questions? Here's the quick answers.

How much does it cost to install a new tap in Hull?
Installing a new tap (kitchen or bathroom) costs £100 to £180 including the labour. That's for a straightforward swap where the pipework's already there. If we need to re-route pipes or fit new isolation valves, add £50 to £100. Price doesn't include the tap itself - you supply that or we can source one for you.
How long does a tap or toilet installation take?
A tap installation takes about an hour if it's a straight swap. A toilet installation takes 1-2 hours depending on whether we need to adjust pipework or fit a new soil pipe connector. Washing machine or dishwasher hookup is usually under an hour.
Do you install washing machines and dishwashers?
Yes. We'll connect the cold water supply, fit the waste hose, level the appliance, and test it. Costs £80 to £140. If you need new isolation valves or the waste pipe re-routing, that's extra. We don't install the appliance if it's built-in cabinetry, that's a kitchen fitter's job.
Can you fit an outside tap?
Yes, outside taps are common jobs. We'll run a pipe from your internal supply (usually the kitchen), drill through the wall, fit the tap, and test it. Costs £120 to £220 depending on how far the pipe run is and what we're drilling through (brick is easy, cavity wall with insulation is trickier).
Do you do full bathroom installations?
No. Full bathroom installs need tilers, electricians, plasterers, sometimes builders. We're plumbers, not bathroom fitters. But if you've had a bathroom fitted and you need a plumber to connect everything up (taps, toilet, shower, bath waste), we can do that.
Can you replace old lead pipes?
Yes, if they're accessible. Lead pipes are usually found running from the boundary stop cock to your internal stop cock. Replacing a short run (1-3 metres) costs £200 to £400. Yorkshire Water will replace the section they own (from the street to your boundary) for free if it's lead, but you're responsible for the section from the boundary into your house.
Do you work on commercial properties?
We focus on domestic work (houses, flats, small rental properties). For commercial plumbing (shops, offices, restaurants), contact us and we'll let you know if we can help or recommend a commercial plumber.
Can you install a shower pump?
Yes, if your water pressure's low and you need a boost for an electric shower or power shower. Shower pumps cost £200 to £400 to install depending on the model and where it's going (usually in the loft or airing cupboard). We'll check your system's compatible before we start.
