Common Questions

Common Questions About Plumbing in Anlaby

Why is my water pressure so low in my Anlaby semi?
1930s properties in Anlaby often have galvanised steel pipes that corrode inside over 80-90 years. The corrosion narrows the pipe bore and restricts flow. You might also have brown or orange water when you first run the tap. We can replace the rising main with modern copper or plastic (£300-£600) or fit a booster pump if the mains pressure itself is weak.
Do you replace galvanised steel pipes in Anlaby?
Yes, we do full repiping jobs across Anlaby. If your 1930s semi still has galvanised steel, it's probably corroded and causing low pressure or discolored water. We replace the rising main, the bathroom supply, and any other sections that are shot. Takes 1-2 days depending on access.
How much does tap repair cost in Anlaby?
Tap repairs in Anlaby (HU10) cost £60-£120. If it's just a washer or cartridge, it's usually closer to £60-£80. If the tap body is corroded and needs replacing, it's £90-£120. We'll check it when we arrive and quote before starting work.
Can you unblock waste pipes in Anlaby properties?
Yes, we clear blocked sinks, showers, and baths all the time in Anlaby. Kitchen sink blockages are usually fat and grease buildup, bathroom sinks are hair and soap scum. We rodent and use drain unblocker, or dismantle the trap if needed. Usually sorted within an hour for £85-£140.
Do post-war estates in Anlaby have different plumbing issues?
The post-war estates (1950s-60s) typically have copper pipes which are better than galvanised steel, but the original fittings are 60-70 years old. We see corroded compression joints, worn tap washers, leaking toilet cisterns, and occasionally burst pipes when they freeze. Less severe than the 1930s properties but stuff still wears out.
How quickly can you get to Anlaby for a plumbing emergency?
We aim to be with you within 2 hours for emergencies like burst pipes or major leaks if you call during business hours (Mon-Thurs 8.30am-6pm, Fri 9am-4pm). Anlaby's close to Hull so we can usually get there quickly.
